🇩🇪 German Alphabet & Pronunciation Cheat Sheet (A1)
 1️⃣ The Alphabet
Letter	Name	Pronunciation Tip / Example
A a	ah	wie in Mann
B b	beh	wie in Buch
C c	tseh	usually in City words, rare
D d	deh	wie in Tag
E e	eh	wie in Bett
F f	eff	wie in Fisch
G g	geh	hard g: Garten; soft g before e/i: Giraffe
H h	hah	pronounced at start: Haus, silent after vowel: sehen
I i	ee	wie in Ich
J j	yot	like English yes → Ja
K k	kah	wie in Karte
L l	ell	wie in Lampe
M m	em	wie in Mutter
N n	en	wie in Name
O o	oh	wie in Sohn
P p	peh	wie in Park
Q q	koo	usually in Quelle
R r	err	rolled or throat r, rot
S s	ess	like s in Sonne; at start before p/t → sh
T t	teh	wie in Tag
U u	oo	wie in gut
V v	fau	usually like f, Vater
W w	veh	like English v, Wasser
X x	iks	like English x, Taxi
Y y	üpsilon	like German ü or sometimes English y
Z z	tsett	like ts, Zeit
🔹 2️⃣ Special Letters / Umlauts
Letter	Sound / Tip	Example
Ä ä	like e in bed	Mädchen
Ö ö	round lips, sound between e and o	schön
Ü ü	round lips, like ee in see	Müll
ß	sharp ss	Straße
🔥 3️⃣ Letter Combinations & Pronunciation Rules
Combination	Sound / Tip	Example
ch	soft after e/i → ich, hard after a/o/u → Buch	ich, Buch
sch	sh	Schule
ei	ai	mein
ie	ee	Sie
au	ow	Haus
eu / äu	oy	neu, Häuser
sp / st	start of word → shp / sht	Spiel, Stadt
