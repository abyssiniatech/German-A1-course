1️⃣ Formal Greetings (for strangers, office, teachers, exams)

Guten Morgen – Good morning

Guten Tag – Good day / Hello (formal)

Guten Abend – Good evening

Auf Wiedersehen – Goodbye

Wie geht es Ihnen? – How are you? (formal)

Freut mich, Sie kennenzulernen – Nice to meet you (formal)

2️⃣ Informal Greetings (friends, peers, young people)

Hallo – Hi / Hello

Hi – Hi

Tschüss – Bye

Bis bald – See you soon

Wie geht's? – How's it going? / How are you? (informal)

Schön dich zu sehen – Nice to see you (informal)

3️⃣ Common Short Responses

Mir geht es gut, danke – I am fine, thank you

Sehr gut – Very good

Es geht – It’s okay / so-so

Nicht so gut – Not so good

4️⃣ Speaking Tips

Use formal greetings in exams, official situations, or when unsure.

Use informal greetings with friends or casual settings.

Practice aloud multiple times, record yourself, and repeat daily.
