1️⃣ Basic Formula

Use this for exams, conversations, or self-practice:

Hallo.
Ich heiße [Your Name].
Ich komme aus [Your Country].
Example
Hallo.
Ich heiße Surafel.
Ich komme aus Äthiopien.
2️⃣ Optional Add-ons (to expand conversation)

Ich bin [Student/Lehrer/etc.] – I am [student/teacher/etc.]

Ich lerne Deutsch. – I am learning German.

Ich spreche ein bisschen Englisch. – I speak a little English.

Mein Hobby ist [Sport/Musik/etc.]. – My hobby is [sport/music/etc.].

3️⃣ Tips for Speaking

Practice aloud 5–10 times slowly, then at normal speed.

Record yourself and compare with DW audio.

Stand in front of a mirror and maintain eye contact with yourself (helps fluency).

Repeat daily — your brain memorizes faster with consistent practice.
