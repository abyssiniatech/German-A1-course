Part 1 – True / False (1–20)

Write True (T) or False (F).

Können means can / able to.

Möchten means must.

In modal verb sentences, the main verb goes at the end.

"Ich kann Deutsch sprechen." means I can speak German.

"Ich möchte Kaffee trinken." means I must drink coffee.

"Du kannst mir helfen." means You can help me.

Modal verbs come first in the sentence.

"Kannst du Deutsch sprechen?" is a question.

"Wir möchten Pizza essen." means We would like to eat pizza.

"Ich kann schwimmen." means I can swim.

"Möchtest du Tee?" means Would you like tea?

"Ich möchte lernen." means I can learn.

Modal verbs change the meaning of another verb.

"Können Sie mir helfen?" is polite German.

"Möchten Sie Kaffee?" means Would you like coffee?

The infinitive verb goes at the end.

"Ich kann Deutsch lernen." means I would like to learn German.

"Wir können heute lernen." means We can study today.

"Du möchtest Kaffee trinken." means You would like coffee.

Modal verbs are important in German A1.
